About Me


Hello, I am Onur Emre, residing in the region known as Cappadocia in Turkey. Since 2020, I have been creating content on YouTube, and in 2021, I began modding for the Farming Simulator series. During this time, I have honed my skills in 3D modeling. Currently, I have developed over 70 mods, 11 of which are cross-platform. As of the moment I am writing this, my mods have surpassed 2.9 million downloads on the official ModHub. I am actively improving my skills in the "HTML5" and Unity.


Also I am a technology enthusiast and a computer game aficionado, closely following the latest developments in the field.
